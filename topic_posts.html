About Us

Pingpong Africa is a platform dedicated to celebrating and enhancing the sport of table tennis across Africa. We provide comprehensive coverage of tournaments, player profiles, training tips, and exclusive interviews, reaching an ever-growing audience of enthusiasts and aspiring players. Our mission is to highlight the diverse talent pool within Africa and inspire a new generation of table tennis athletes.
